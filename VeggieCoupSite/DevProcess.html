Playtesting

In total we have had three major play test sessions, an Alpha, Beta, and Final. Our major notes and eventual changes that came from these sessions are shown below.

Alpha

  • Players confused by no world boundaries
  • The enemy spawner is too close to the farm and should be moved further away.
  • Add UI for weapons so players know what weapons they have and what order they are in.
  • Add accelerated player movement rather than instant constant velocity for more natural movement.
  • Players are getting caught off guard by running out of ammo, so the ammo UI should make it more obvious when the player is low on ammo.
  • Players are having a difficult time telling when the crops have changed to a new growth state, so crop state changes should be more apparent.
  • Add a lose condition.

Beta

  • Add additional levels for more challenge and replayability.
  • Win/Lose message on game over screen so players know if they won.
  • Collision on the gun so it doesn’t poke through enemies.
  • Increase shotgun range so that the gun is better.
  • Border for the minimap to make it easier to distinguish from the background.
  • Pause Menu so players can take a break and/or look up the controls during the game.
  • Health bars for enemies so players know if their shots are connecting and how close enemies are to dying.

Final

  • Provide more clear differences between levels.
  • Make the timer a progress bar rather than a number.
  • Instead of ammo text, have a potato image (andres)
  • Hold down buttons to  plant/harvest so that players don’t have to button spam.
  • I-frames after knockback so that players aren’t hit multiple times by a bunch of enemies stacked on each other.
