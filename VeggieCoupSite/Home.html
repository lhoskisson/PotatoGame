Game Description

Veggie-Coup is  a top down shooter survival game. It's up to you to defend your potato crops from the rest of the vegetables from your farm that want to overthrow you. Veggie-Coup's challenge comes from the importance of potato management. Potato crops grow over time and can be harvested for more potatoes when fully grown. Planting potato crops costs ammo and so does firing your potato gun. If you run out of potato crops and potatoes, you lose. If you can defend all three of your potato farms, you win!
